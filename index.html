The Story

The increasing amount of debris orbiting Earth could potentially limit our access to space, impacting not only exploration efforts, but routine aspects of our life on Earth. This web app is a response to the NASA Space apps challenge: to develop an open-source geospatial application that displays and locates every known debris object orbiting Earth in real time.
